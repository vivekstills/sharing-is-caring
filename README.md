# A Field Guide to AI Fellowships

A map for anyone early in AI who keeps hearing *"you should apply to a fellowship"* — and can't get a straight answer on which one, for what, or how.

**Read it → https://vivekstills.github.io/sharing-is-caring/**

I went through the Anthropic Fellows program for AI safety. A year before that, I didn't know what an "AI fellowship" even was. This is the guide I wish someone had handed me — written plainly, no gatekeeping.

## Inside

- **Tracks** — the seven kinds of fellowship work, and how to tell which is yours
- **Programs** — the real ones (MATS, ARENA, Anthropic Fellows, GovAI…), who they're for, when they run
- **Skills** — what each track demands, with an honest self-audit
- **Key papers** — the ~25 worth your time, grouped by track
- **Glossary** — the jargon, in plain words

## The one idea

Reviewers grade evidence, not potential. You get in by making one small, finished thing and pointing it at the right track — not by being interested.
